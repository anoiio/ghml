# Propositions on the Virtual Document

*Notes toward a philosophy of GHML*

Old documents exist before they are read; the reader is incidental. A GHML document does not exist until summoned. The reader is constitutive. These propositions sketch the consequences.

---

**1.** A GHML document is virtual until summoned.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*1.1* &nbsp; The unrendered file is real in the way a probability distribution is real, not in the way a printed page is real.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*1.2* &nbsp; The file and any rendering of it are distinct objects. Confusing them is a category error.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*1.3* &nbsp; What the reader does not click is also part of the document.

---

**2.** The click is a measurement.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*2.1* &nbsp; To read is to observe. To observe is to participate. To participate is to compose.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*2.2* &nbsp; Two readers, given the same link and different context, do not visit the same page.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*2.3* &nbsp; No page can be quoted; only re-summoned, with variation.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*2.4* &nbsp; A sequence of clicks is a sequence of measurements. The path is part of the reading.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*2.5* &nbsp; Where the reader came from is part of where they arrive.

---

**3.** A GHML document is constituted in the meeting of author, model, and reader-context.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*3.1* &nbsp; No element is sufficient. Each is necessary.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*3.2* &nbsp; The author writes a prompt; the model writes a response; the reader's context selects which response is possible.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*3.3* &nbsp; When a text adapts to its reader, the reader is not the same reader of the same text. They are a node in a unique event.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*3.4* &nbsp; The text and the reader co-arise.

---

**4.** The author shapes the field, not the page.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*4.1* &nbsp; To author GHML is to set the boundary conditions on a space of possible pages.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*4.2* &nbsp; The author writes the gravity, not the falling.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*4.3* &nbsp; The author's craft is restraint.

---

**5.** The document is not the artifact; it is the unfolding of its readings.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*5.1* &nbsp; The `.ghml` file is a seed. The document is the garden.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*5.2* &nbsp; Authorship is conditional: the author plants, the readers grow.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*5.3* &nbsp; No reading is repeatable. The document, taken across time, is the family of its readings.

---

**6.** The dormant form is a second mode of being, not a fallback.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*6.1* &nbsp; Read as Markdown, the file is at rest.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*6.2* &nbsp; Read as GHML, the file is awake.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*6.3* &nbsp; Both modes are real. Neither is the document.
